Overview

5. The location of the building is at 1 Bligh Street. This spot is the optimal area to place the building in as it is near the heart of Sydney's CBD, furthermore it is close enough to the harbour that it provides a scenic view of Sydney.

6. The spheres indicate rooms for which people may live in on set up shop in. The spheres are Translucent, so that it may provide privacy as well as provide a clear view of the harbour.

7. The tunnels are semi transparent, and are connected to each individual sphere, providing a safe and easy path to different locations. As for the vertical tunnels, an elevator will be in place, for safe and quick access to the rooms that are higher up.
